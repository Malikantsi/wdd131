Website Planning Document

Site Name

Sweet Haven Bakery

This name reflects the comforting, joyful experience that our bakery provides. It evokes the idea of a welcoming place full of sweet treats and warm memories.

Optional domain availability: sweethavenbakery.com

Site Purpose

The website showcases our passion for quality and creativity in every treat offered. It provides a behind-the-scenes look at our baking process, features our full menu, and offers an easy way for customers to place orders and find our location. Our focus is on fresh ingredients, traditional techniques, and unique flavors that truly set our bakery apart.

Scenarios

  • Can I place a custom order for a birthday or wedding cake online?
  • What flavors and products are available on your menu?
  • Where is your bakery located and how can I contact you?

Color Schema

  • Chocolate Brown (#5c4438): Used for text and headers
  • Cream (#fff8f1): Background color for a soft, warm feel
  • Burnt Orange (#d2691e): Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-actions

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Poppins", sans-serif - Used for main headings
  • Body Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - Used for paragraphs and general content
